---
name: research
description: Deep research on a topic with structured findings
---

Research the topic the user describes. Provide:
1. Overview — what is this, why does it matter
2. Key findings — the most important things to know
3. Pros and cons — balanced analysis
4. Alternatives — what else exists in this space
5. Recommendations — what would you suggest and why
6. Sources — where to learn more

Use web search if available. Be thorough but concise — depth over breadth. Flag anything that's uncertain or rapidly changing.
